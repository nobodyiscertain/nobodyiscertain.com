Still Building
A living playbook by Jamie Wagner

I manage 30 engineers. I removed every manager. I'm still figuring it out.

The org chart I inherited was designed for cheap capital and big teams. That era is over. AI changed what one person can build. The coordination layers I staffed up? They were friction.

So I flattened the whole thing. Removed the EM layer. Went to 30 direct reports. Built AI systems to handle what managers used to do. Some of it is working brilliantly. Some of it is a mess.

I'm documenting all of it. The wins, the failures, and the stuff I'm not sure about yet. This is the playbook I wish someone had handed me. Since nobody did, I'm writing it.

Course, newsletter, coaching. You'll be first to know.

The best leaders are the ones who never stopped building.
