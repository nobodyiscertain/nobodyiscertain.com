Description

Sound & Color is the second studio album by American band Alabama Shakes, released on April 17, 2015, by ATO Records. The album debuted at number one on the Billboard 200 in the U.S., giving the band their first chart-topper. It was nominated for six Grammy Awards, winning four for Best Alternative Music Album, Best Engineered Album, Non-Classical, as well as Best Rock Performance and Best Rock Song for "Don't Wanna Fight."
