How Cahoots works

Everyone on your team shows up in a simple grid. Periodic webcam snapshots (every 10-20 seconds) create ambient awareness — you can see who's around without asking.

See someone available? Click their tile. Grayscale snapshot transitions to live color video. You're connected. No calendar invite, no Zoom link, no "can you hear me?" Just a conversation.
